# 🤲 Dhikr

**A voice-powered Islamic dhikr counter that listens and counts automatically.**

> Say it. It counts.

---

## 💡 The Idea

Most dhikr apps require you to tap a button every time you say a remembrance.

This breaks your focus and interrupts the flow of worship.

**Dhikr** solves this by allowing you to recite naturally while the app listens, recognizes your dhikr, and counts automatically.

✅ No tapping  
✅ No interruptions  
✅ Just you and your worship

---

## ✨ How It Works

### 1️⃣ Choose Your Dhikr

Pick the remembrance you want to focus on:

| Arabic | Transliteration | Meaning |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ | Subhanallahi wa bihamdihi | Glory and praise be to Allah |
| الْحَمْدُ لِلَّه | Alhamdulillah | All praise is due to Allah |
| اللهُ أَكْبَر | Allahu Akbar | Allah is the Greatest |
| أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّه | Astaghfirullah | I seek forgiveness from Allah |
| لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّه | La ilaha illallah | There is no god but Allah |

### 2️⃣ Set Your Target

Choose how many times you want to recite:

- 33 — Classic Tasbih Count
- 99 — Extended Session
- 100 — Round Number
- 500 — Long Session
- Unlimited — No Limit
- Custom — Enter Your Own Number

### 3️⃣ Choose Your Notification Sound

Pick the sound you want to hear when you reach your target.

## 🔒 Privacy First

All audio is processed entirely on your device.

- No audio is stored.
- No data is sent to servers.
- No recordings are shared.

Your privacy remains protected at all times.
